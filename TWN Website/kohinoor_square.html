A decade positioning and marketing India's tallest commercial tower

About Project

Kohinoor Square is the one of the tallest mixed-use development projects in the country. The property is now ready and stands as one of the iconic landmarks at the center of the city at Dadar. The project houses the tallest commercial tower at 55-storeys, a luxury residential tower, 5 floors of retail, and 10 floors of community parking. The project is 75% sold, and Think WhyNot has handled the entire mandate, right from it’s inception to it’s turnkey sales till date.

Challenge

The challenge in such a large project is to have multiple campaigns in phases as the inventory gets sold in blocks. The challenge in Kohinoor Square was that of positioning the project and tackling the sales of commercial, residential and the retail units. To complicate things, the management changed hands thrice, there was a lull in the construction activity and as if this was not enough, we went through an intensive pandemic.

Evolution of the Marketing and Sales Journey: 

Phase 1:

The property began with an ultra-luxurious positioning of being one of Mumbai’s top luxury real estate landmarks. The property was launched in the market through whisper marketing - An exlcusive outreach to the top 1000 CXOs through a tie-up with the Forbes magazine and sponsorship of the Forbes India Leadership Awards. 

The collaterals were built through an art collaboration with some of Mumbai’s top artists. The customer experience at the property was enhanced through augmented reality and state of the art experience zones.

We next went to market with an above the line (ATL) campaign with the commercial inventory targeting high network businessmen for small office spaces. Thanks to the initial build up and a sharper campaign, we managed to sell 50% of the targeted inventory. 

Then the management changed hands, and construction came to a halt. 

Phase 2:

The project was restructured with fresh funding, and Think WhyNot was invited again by the new management to pitch for the renewed marketing efforts. Because of proximity to the project and early involvement, we won the mandate easily and worked on the campaign to launch the commercial and residential towers. A new campaign was designed, new collaterals made, around the positioning of "___". 

Covid struck and everything went into a freeze. However, the silver lining was everyone realised that the only safe place in Covid was their own home. This gave us our new campaign idea - “Home: The Center Of The New Normal”. This campaign was launched just as things opened up.

Phase 3:

Mopping up sales. By now, the property was half-ready. Any lingering negative perceptions about the property were fast getting erased. We launched two campaigns in quick succession. The first one highlighted how Dadar as a neighborhood was getting upgraded thanks to the presence of Kohinoor.

We realised that most of our sales were coming from the neighborhood and hence we activated the neighborhood sentiment through a straight forward testimonial cmapaign. The campaign was titled "Happy To Be Here". 

We still handle the account and are working to liquidate the last 20% of the inventory.
